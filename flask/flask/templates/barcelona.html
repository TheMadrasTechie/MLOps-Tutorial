FC Barcelona

More Than a Club

The Pride of Catalonia

FC Barcelona, also known as Barça, is one of the most iconic football clubs in the world. Founded in 1899, it has become a symbol of Catalan pride and independence. Known for its distinct playing style, a rich history of success, and legendary players like Lionel Messi, Ronaldinho, and Johan Cruyff, FC Barcelona has won numerous domestic and international titles. The club's motto, "Més que un club" (More than a club), reflects its deep connection with the Catalan community, culture, and the global football world.
